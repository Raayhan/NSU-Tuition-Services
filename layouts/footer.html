About NSU-TS

NORTH SOUTH UNIVERSITY TUITION SERVICES

NSU-TS is a platform for NSU Students to find suitable tutors for the courses which they find difficulties.It's also a platform for students who want to earn some money by giving tuitions to other students.

Student

Student can register as a student in NSU-TS to use services. They can browse department wise course or can see currently active courses offered by Tutors. After getting desired course tutors they can add that course into their course list in order to get connected with them. However they can always remove a course from their profile whenever they want.

Teacher

Teachers can register as a teacher in NSU-TS to use the services. they can add courses from dashboard panel and check status of active sourses and current student lists. Whenever a new student connects it shows up in the student list.
